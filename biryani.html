Biryani

Instructions

  1. Skin chicken and cut into large pieces (with bones).
  2. Wash rice and leave to soak.
  3. Peel garlic and mince. Peel ginger and mince. Peel onions and chop into medium pieces.
  4. Put all of these into a large cooking pot. Add one cup of oil and cook on high heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. After about 15 minutes, add the pieces of chicken.
  6. Add 3 full spoons of biryani masala, 2 spoons salt, 2 spoons of red chili pepper, and a spoon of turmeric.
  7. After 10 minutes add diced tomatoes.
  8. After another 10 minutes when the mixture has become less watery, add 1 cup of plain yogurt.
  9. Cook for about 20 more minutes or until mixture is thick, stirring occasionally.
  10. Its done when the mixture is very thick and not watery; make sure the chicken is cooked through.
  11. Cook about 6 cups of rice.
  12. In a pot, put one layer of the biryani masala that you cooked and then add a layer of rice, continue to do this until everything is layered.
  13. Mix with spoon about three times. Add cilantro and jalapenos on top.

Ingredients

  • 6 onions
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 10 cloves of garlic
  • 1 inch piece of ginger
  • 1 chicken
  • 2 tbsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp ground red chili pepper
  • ½ tbsp turmeric
  • ½ box of Shan Masala Sindi Biryani Spice mix
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 5 to 6 cups basmati rice (or long grain rice)
  • 1 bunch cilantro (optional)
  • 2 jalapeños (optional)
  • 5 dried plums (optional)
