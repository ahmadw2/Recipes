Seviyan

Instructions

  1. Cut seviyan in four pieces and place in pot on stove on medium heat with oil.
  2. Stir until all seviyan are coated in oil.
  3. Make sure seviyan do not burn, if needed, turn off stove while stirring.
  4. Add milk and cook until desired thickness
  5. Add sugar
  6. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes
  7. Add almonds and kewra essence
  8. Add crushed pistachios for garnish

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Seviyan
  • 1/2 gallon milk
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 10 peeled and crushed green cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp kewra essence
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 5 pistachios
