Hot Lava Cake

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Butter 5 ramekins
  3. Microwave chocolate and butter in large microwaveable bowl until butter is melted; whisk until chocolate is completely melted.
  4. Stir in sugar.
  5. Add whole eggs and egg yolks; mix well.
  6. Stir in flour.
  7. Spoon into prepared cups.
  8. Bake 8 to 10 min or until sides of desserts are firm but centers are still soft. Let stand 1 min.
  9. Carefully run small spatula around cakes to loosen; invert into dessert plates. Serve with vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. (4oz) Ghirardelli semi sweet chocolate bar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 6 tbsp flour
  • Optional: vanilla ice cream
