Creme Brulee

Instructions

  1. Scrape vanilla bean and place seeds and bean in heavy cream.
  2. Heat in saucepan while stirring, until not quite boiled, until film forms on top.
  3. Remove from heat and let sit 15 minutes, covered. Strain.
  4. Beat 2 egg yolks and 1/8 cup sugar (2 Tbsp).
  5. Add some cream to the mixture and stir. Little by little add all the cream.
  6. Pour cream mixture into ramekins and pop bubbles.
  7. Place ramekins in a baking dish and pour hot water around creme brulee dish until its half way up the creme brulee dishes.
  8. Bake at 325 degrees for 35-45 minutes until the creme brulee jiggles like jello.
  9. Remove from oven and cool in water bath for 15 minutes.
  10. Chill in refrigerator.
  11. Top with white sugar evenly and then burn sugar with torch until golden. Serve soon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 vanilla bean
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • white sugar for sprinkling
