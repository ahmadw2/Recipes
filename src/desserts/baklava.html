Baklava

Instructions

  1. Make sure phyllo dough is fully thawed.
  2. Mix nuts, sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  4. Melt one stick of butter until it is liquid.
  5. Take phyllo dough sheets and place in pan.
  6. Trim excess dough off the sides.
  7. Fold one side of the sheets over to the other sandwich style.
  8. Fold back one layer of the phyllo dough and brush with butter.
  9. After each five (or however many you decide), sprinkle evenly with nut mixture, make sure the sheet you sprinkle is also buttered.
  10. Repeat layers until all done.
  11. Sprinkle nuts on top of finished baklava.
  12. Take pizza cutter and cut slices.
  13. You can cut diamonds or squares, whichever is easier.
  14. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
  15. Take it out when top is golden brown and before the nuts turn dark.

Sauce:

  1. Mix sugar, water, honey and lemon juice in a saucepan.
  2. Heat on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes or until the mixture is syrupy.
  3. Keep stirring or the sugar will burn.
  4. When baklava is done, let it cool for a bit so its not hot, then pour sauce evenly over the baklava with a spoon.
  5. Let the baklava cool before putting it in a dish, the syrup acts like glue as it cools and keeps the baklava intact.
  6. You might need to cut the pieces with a knife to help separate them before removing them from the pan.

Ingredients

  • Phyllo dough
  • 3 cups chopped nuts (mix of almonds, walnuts/pecans, peanuts, pistachios, hazelnuts)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter (can add more if needed)

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup water
