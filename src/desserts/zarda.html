Zarda

Instructions

  1. Boil rice in open water.
  2. Add zarda food coloring
  3. Strain rice
  4. Place butter, oil and green cardamom in pan. Cook and stir occasionally until fragrant
  5. Add 1/2 cup water and sugar and stir to keep from crystalizing
  6. When mixture starts to boil, add rice
  7. Add almonds and optional garnishes such as orange peel
  8. Cook until water evaporates
  9. Cook for another 10 minutes on low heat

Ingredients

  • 2 cup rice
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 8 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 10 green cardamom
  • 20 Sliced almonds
  • 1/2 tsp Zarda food coloring
  • Optional: dried pineapple, dried fruit, orange peel, cashew
