Kheer

Instructions

  1. 1 mug rice, soak 30 minutes
  2. Boil rice in a little water ( 3 mugs) on full heat
  3. Break up rice when tender
  4. Add half and half and other milk
  5. Cook, add sugar, taste
  6. Add laichee powder
  7. Add pistachios
  8. Add rose water or few keura

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Half and half
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Laichee powder
  • Pistachios
  • Rose water
