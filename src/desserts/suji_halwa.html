Suji Halwa

Instructions

  1. Place suji, butter, cardamom on stove on low heat until light brown
  2. In separate pot, boil 2 cups milk and 1 cup water
  3. Carefully add milk and water in the suji
  4. When mixture is dry, add sugar
  5. Cook on medium to high heat until mixture is dry
  6. Add almonds
  7. Cook on low heat for 5 minutes
  8. Suji can be cooked faster without butter in steel pan.
  9. Add butter after suji is light brown.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (Suji)
  • 8 Tbsp butter
  • 8 peeled and crushed green cardamom
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 handful of crushed almonds
