Badam Ka Sharbat

Instructions

  1. Soak 1/2 cup almonds
  2. Peel and grind them into fine paste
  3. Soak 1 tbsp poppy and 1 tbsp melon seeds for 2 hours. Make a fine paste by grinding.
  4. Make 1 sugar syrup by boiling 3 cups sugar and 2 cups water together. Add all pastes and 8 green cardamoms to sugar syrup and let boil on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Let syrup cool and mix 1/4 cup sharbat with chilled water or milk!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1 tbsp poppy
  • 1 tbsp melon seeds
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 8 green cardamoms
