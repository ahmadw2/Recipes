Lassi

Instructions

  1. Whisk/blend 1 cup yogurt
  2. Add 3 cups water
  3. Whisk/blend water and yogurt
  4. Add 1/2 tsp salt
  5. For meethi lassi replace salt with 4 tbsp sugar
  6. Serve over ice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Ice

Serving size: 2
