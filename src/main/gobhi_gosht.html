Gobhi Gosht

Instructions

  1. Combine goat, onion, oil, garlic, tomato, and spices in pressure cooker.
  2. Pressure cook 5 minutes on high (or 30 mins without pressure).
  3. Meanwhile cut cauliflower.
  4. After cooking, mix and reduce water.
  5. Add cauliflower and ginger, reduce further.
  6. Optional: split mixture into 2 pots for larger servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb goat meat
  • 1 bunch cauliflower
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1½ tsp red chili pepper
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 inch ginger
  • ½ cup oil
