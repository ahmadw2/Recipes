Kichri

Instructions

  1. Soak moong daal for 1 hour and rinse
  2. Soak rice for 1/2 hour
  3. Slice onions in long slim pieces
  4. Add onions and oil in pot and cook until brown
  5. Add four cups water
  6. When water starts to boil, add daal, salt, black peppercorns and cinnamon stick
  7. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes
  8. Check to see if daal is softened
  9. Add rice and cumin seeds
  10. Cover and occasionally stir
  11. When water evaporates, stir entire pot.Cook for another 10 minutes on low heat.
