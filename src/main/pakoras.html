Pakoras

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients into a batter with water.
  2. Heat oil in deep pan.
  3. Drop spoonfuls into oil and fry until crispy and golden.
  4. Serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped spinach
  • 6 green onions
  • 2 red potatoes
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 small eggplants
  • 2 jalapeños
  • 2 tsp red chili pepper
  • 2½ tsp salt
  • 3 cups chickpea flour
  • 3 tsp crushed coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
