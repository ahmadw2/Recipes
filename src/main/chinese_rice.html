Chinese Rice

Instructions

  1. Make 1 cup of rice
  2. Saute onion, green onion, and jalapeño in pan and add to rice
  3. Add oil
  4. Add soy sauce and pepper to taste
  5. Whisk eggs in bowl
  6. Cook eggs in pan, add salt and pepper
  7. Add eggs to rice and stir

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled rice
  • 1/2 sautéed onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 sautéed green onions
  • 1 jalapeno
  • Oil
