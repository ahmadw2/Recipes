Chicken Corn Soup

Instructions

  1. Fill 3/4 of a pot with water and add chicken. Boil for 1/2 hour
  2. Remove chicken from water and place in bowl. Wait until chicken is cool to remove chicken from bones. Make small pieces of chicken with hands.
  3. Place all bones in the water and cook on medium heat for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Take out 2 cups of broth to cook corn. Cook corn for 10 minutes on low heat in broth.
  5. Chop corn in blender or use hand held blender.
  6. Strain the broth to make sure no bones are left. Add chopped corn mixture.
  7. Add chicken, vinegar, soy sauce, black pepper, salt.
  8. In bowl, whisk eggs with fork.
  9. When water is still boiling, pour eggs into the water while stirring the soup.
  10. Mix cornstarch in 1 cup cold water and add to soup.
  11. Add more vinegar, soy sauce and salt to taste.
  12. For thicker soup, add more corn starch.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
