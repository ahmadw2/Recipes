Kaalay Channay

Instructions

  1. Soak channay all day/night.
  2. Add channay and 4 cups water into pot with onion, garlic, and spices.
  3. Evaporate water.
  4. Add 12 cups water and cook until tender.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups kaalay channay
  • 1 onion
  • 7 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp red chili pepper
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
