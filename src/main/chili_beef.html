Chili Beef

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and black pepper.
  2. Fry ginger and garlic slightly in oil
  3. Add beef and fry.
  4. Fry jalepenos separately and stir in.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef
  • 2 2/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp black paper
  • 1-2 jalepenos seeded and julienned
  • 1 tbsp papaya
