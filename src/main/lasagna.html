Lasagna

Instructions

  1. Wash the ground beef
  2. Mix ground beef with a bit of water and oil to separate texture
  3. Place ground beef in pressure cooker on stove on medium to high heat
  4. Add garlic, salt, red pepper, turmeric, and cumin seeds
  5. Stir occasionally for five minutes
  6. Cook in pressure cooker for 10 minutes
  7. Mince onion and green bell pepper and mix in bowl
  8. Release the pressure of the cooker and stir occasionally on medium heat until all water has evaporated from the ground beef
  9. Add pasta sauce and stir for five minutes on medium heat
  10. Boil lasagna in water and strain
  11. Use a 11"x14" inch baking dish to layer lasagna with beef and mozzarella cheese in every layer. Make 4 layers and top with mozzarella.
  12. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 lasagna box
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 16 to 24 oz cottage cheese
  • 1 minced onion
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
