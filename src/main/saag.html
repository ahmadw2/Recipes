Saag

Instructions

  1. Cook spinach, broccoli, mustard, jalapeño, and garlic with water.
  2. Fry shredded garlic and ginger in oil until golden.
  3. Blend greens and return to pot with oil.
  4. Adjust seasoning and serve.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chopped spinach
  • 1 lb chopped broccoli
  • 1 lb chopped mustard greens
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 2 inch ginger
  • 1 tsp red chili pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 jalapeños
  • ½ bunch cilantro (optional)
  • ¾ cup oil
