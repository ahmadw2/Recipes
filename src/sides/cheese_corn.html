Cheese Corn

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Combine all ingredients into a mixing bowl and stir together.
  3. Pour mixture into a lightly greased baking dish.
  4. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until mixture melts and bubbles.
  5. Remove from oven and cool for about 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears fresh sweet corn kernels
  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste
