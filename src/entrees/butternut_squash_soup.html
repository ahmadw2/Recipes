Butternut Squash Soup

Instructions

  1. Saute onions in butter
  2. Add broth and squash
  3. Pressure cooker 15-20 minutes
  4. Blend in blender with cream cheese
  5. Season with salt and black pepper

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, small or medium chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • 32 oz or 4 cups butternut squash chopped
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • sprinkle paprika
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
