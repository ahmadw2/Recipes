Aaloogosht

Ingredients

  • 1 lb goat meat
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1½ tsp red chili pepper
  • ½ tsp of turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 inch piece of ginger
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Cut fat off goat meat and cut into medium sized pieces. Put goat, onions, oil, garlic, tomatoes, spices (salt, pepper, turmeric, cumin) into pressure cooker.
  2. Pressure cook for five minutes on high heat. If you dont have pressure cooker, leave on medium heat for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cut and wash potatoes into small pieces.
  4. After five minutes of pressure, open lid and mix. Evaporate water a little bit before adding in the potatoes*.
  5. Add potatoes into mixture and 4 cups of water. Add ginger last.
  6. After potatoes are cooked, put lid on and leave on low heat for five minutes.

*Note: If you make larger serving, take all liquid mixture out of the pressure cooker, leaving only the meat, and place the liquid into another pot to evaporate the water. Keep cooking the meat mixture until you see pools of oil and the mixture is sticking to the meat. Meanwhile cook the potatoes in the liquid mixture in another pot, mixing from time to time. Add ginger after some water has evaporated. Combine both pots and mix.
