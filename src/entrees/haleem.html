Haleem

Instructions

  1. Soak Daal for 4 hours or overnight
  2. Rinse
  3. Put daal in pressure cooker and fill half with water
  4. Cook in pressure cooker for 10 minutes
  5. In another pressure cooker, add 1/2 cup oil and steak. Stir for 10 minutes until steak is a light brown.
  6. Add 4 cups water and cook in pressure cooker for 10 minutes.
  7. In another pot, place beef bones and 6 cups water. Cook on medium heat for one hour.
  8. Remove bones from water.
  9. Place daal and steak in chopper.
  10. Mix daal, steak and broth all in one pot. Add full packet of Shan masala. Cook and stir occasionally on low heat until done. Add more masala according to taste.
  11. Cook 2 onions in 1/2 cup oil in pan until crispy golden brown.
  12. Garnish with onions, chopped jalape√±os, chopped cilanto, lime, and ginger.

Ingredients

  • Haleem Mix Packet (Daal)
  • Haleem Shan Masala
  • 2 lbs boneless steak
  • 1 lb Beef bones
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2 onions
  • 2 inch ginger piece
  • 2 Jalapeno
  • 1 bunch Cilantro
  • 2 limes
