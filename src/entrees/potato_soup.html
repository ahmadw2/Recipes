Potato Soup

Instructions

  1. Place ingredients in pot and cover with half a pot of water.
  2. Boil until potatoes are soft (15-20 minutes).
  3. Add in 1 1/2 vegetable or chicken bullion cubes and stir. Taste. Add salt and pepper if needed.
  4. Before this next step, make sure the milk is out, measured and ready to add.
  5. In a saucepan, melt 2 tbsp butter on low heat and mix in 3 tbsp (9 tsp) flour and whisk in until creamy and custard like.
  6. Stir in 1 1/2 cup milk and cook until thickened, making sure no clumps form. Use a whisk.
  7. Add this to vegetable soup and stir.
  8. Taste soup for salt/pepper and serve when creamy.
  9. You can increase or decrease veggies, milk, seasoning etc.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups potatos diced small
  • 2 large celery stalks diced
  • 1 large carrot sliced thin
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 1 1/2 vegetable or chicken builllon cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp (9 tsp) flour
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
