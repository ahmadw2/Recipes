Spanish Chicken Potatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F.
  2. Microwave potatoes with garlic, oil, and water for 8 minutes.
  3. Season chicken with paprika, salt, pepper, herbs, and lemon juice.
  4. Roast potatoes and onions for 12 mins, then add chicken.
  5. Roast until chicken is cooked and potatoes are browned.
  6. Top with parsley and serve with lemon wedges and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Yukon gold potatoes
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2–3 sprigs fresh dill
  • Black pepper
  • 4 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 lemons
  • 2–3 red onions
  • Sour cream (for serving)
