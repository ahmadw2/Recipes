Bhindi

Instructions

  1. Put okra in a pan. Add onions and oil. Place on high heat, stirring occasionally.
  2. After 5 to 10 minutes, add chopped tomatoes to pan.
  3. Add red chili pepper, salt and turmeric.
  4. Leave for 20 minutes.
  5. It is done when the water evaporates and oil starts to separate.
  6. At the end, you can squeeze a lime or lemon on top.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups okra
  • 4 onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • ¾ cup oil
  • 1 tsp red chili pepper
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lemon or lime
