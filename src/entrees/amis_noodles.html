Ami's Noodles

Instructions

  1. Boil water for noodles, once it starts boiling, add two tablespoons oil and two tablespoons salt
  2. Cook for 12 minutes
  3. Drain and put cold water on noodles to prevent from sticking
  4. Cut chicken into cubes
  5. Add lemon juice , 1/6 cup soy sauce, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp ground black pepper into bowl with chicken
  6. Mix and then add one tablespoon of cornstarch
  7. Cut celery
  8. Mix stirfry, and peas into pan
  9. Cook for 10 minutes
  10. Add celery. Cook for five minutes Add spinach and cook for a few more minutes
  11. Add 2 tablespoons soy sauce to vegetables and 2 tablespoons vinegar
  12. Meanwhile, fill pan with oil and put chicken in a pan to cook. About 8 minutes
  13. Put noodles in large nonstick pot and add oil. Mix noodles and make sure they separate. Mix veggies with noodles.
  14. Add 1/4 cup soy sauce. Add 1/4 cup vinegar.
  15. Mix chicken into noodles. Add one tsp black ground pepper and one tsp salt.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of spaghetti noodles
  • 1/6 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbs vinegar
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1/8 cup Lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 packet stirfry
  • 3 sticks celery
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1-2 cup baby spinach
