Southwest Sandwich

Instructions

  1. Blend southwest sauce ingredients in chopper.
  2. Marinate chicken with garlic, ground cumin, ground chili powder, limes, lime juice, oil and cilantro.
  3. In pan, sear chicken on each side for three minutes.
  4. Assemble sandwiches with toasted ciabatta bread, chicken, southwest sauce, cheese, avocado and tomato.

Ingredients

Southwest Sauce

  • 1/4 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream

Chicken Marination

  • 1 1/2 lb chicken
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • 1 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 Tbsp ground chili powder
  • 2 limes zest and juice
  • 1 cup virgin oil
  • 1/4 cup cilantro

Toppings

  • Ciabatta
  • Choice of sliced cheese
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
