Daal

Instructions

  1. Wash lentils and leave to soak.
  2. Boil 4 mugs water in a pot.
  3. Add lentils and all spices and aromatics.
  4. Leave on high heat for about 30 minutes.
  5. Add ¼ cup oil and cook for 5–10 more minutes.
  6. Taste and serve with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1½ mug red split lentils
  • 2 mugs rice
  • 1 tsp red chili pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • ¼ cup oil
