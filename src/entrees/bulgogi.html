Bulgogi

Instructions

  1. Place the beef in a shallow dish. Combine soy sauce, sugar, green onion, garlic, and ground black pepper in a small bowl. Pour over beef. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Preheat grill or pan on high heat, and lightly oil.
  3. Quickly grill beef until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup crushed pear
  • 1/4 cup onion puree
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 chopped green onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • black pepper
