Crepes

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, mix eggs, vanilla and butter.
  2. Add milk to dry ingredients and mix. Add egg mixture.
  3. Pour about 1/4 cup batter into medium hot, buttered pan.
  4. Turn when barely brown.
  5. Fill with jam, powdered sugar, vanilla ice cream, whip cream, strawberries or anything else you desire!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
