Dutch Baby Pancakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Combine eggs, flour, milk, sugar, and nutmeg in a blender jar and blend until smooth. Butter may also be mixed by hand.
  3. Place butter in a heavy 10 inch skillet or baking dish and place in the oven.
  4. As soon as the butter has melted (watch it so it does not burn) add batter to the pan, return pan to the oven and bake fore 20 minutes, until the pancake is puffed and golden.
  5. Lower oven temperature to 300 degrees and bake five minutes longer.
  6. Remove pancake from oven, cut into wedges and serve at once topped with butter, lemon juice, and powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 lemon
  • Powdered sugar
