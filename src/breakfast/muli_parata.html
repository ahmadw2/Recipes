Muli Parata

Instructions

  1. Squeeze water out of daikon and mix with spices.
  2. Knead roti dough with flour, water, and salt.
  3. Roll out two rotis and butter one.
  4. Place daikon filling and cover with second roti.
  5. Seal edges and cook on high heat until done.
