Seekh Kabob

Instructions

  1. Grind aromatics and bread in food processor.
  2. Mix everything into ground beef and knead.
  3. Shape into kabobs and bake at 500°F for 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium onions
  • 6 jalapeños
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1½ cup garlic
  • 10 tbsp ginger
  • 3 pieces bread (optional)
  • 3 bunches green onion
  • 7 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4 tsp red pepper
  • 7 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 4 tbsp crushed coriander
  • 4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 7 lbs ground beef
  • 3 eggs
