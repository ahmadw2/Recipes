Aaloogosht

Ingredients

  • ¼ finely chopped medium onion
  • ½ tomato finely chopped
  • ½ jalapeno finely chopped
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ crushed cucumber
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups plain yogurt

Instructions

Mix ingredients in a bowl. Place in serving dish after mixing.
