Palau

Instructions

  1. Boil water with meat, onion, garlic, and spices.
  2. Brown remaining onion and garlic in a second pot.
  3. Add boiled meat to browned onion mixture.
  4. Add cooking water and rice, season again.
  5. Simmer until water is gone; finish on low for 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups rice
  • 1 lb chicken or goat
  • 1¼ onion
  • ⅓ inch ginger
  • 9 garlic cloves
  • 2 black cardamom
  • 6 black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 5 cloves
  • 3 whole red pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin
