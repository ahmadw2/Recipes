Chicken Roast

Instructions

  1. Make small cuts with knife on drumsticks.
  2. Toss chicken with all spices.
  3. Add yogurt and toss again until even layer on all chicken.
  4. Bake and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 24 drumsticks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 spoons red chili pepper
  • 3 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
