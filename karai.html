Karai

Instructions

  1. Add meat, onion, garlic, ginger, and spices to pot.
  2. Cook on high, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add tomatoes and oil after water reduces.
  4. Continue until water evaporates and oil separates (about 45 min to 1 hr).
  5. Serve with naan or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken or 2 lbs goat
  • 2 onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 2 inches ginger
  • 2 tsp red chili pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 cup oil
  • 15 cloves
  • 15 whole black peppers
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 black cardamom
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1 bunch cilantro
