Butter Chicken

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop 3 onions.
  2. Peel and mince 6 pieces of garlic.
  3. Put this all in a pot and add ½ cup oil.
  4. Cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Add 4 chopped tomatoes.
  6. Leave covered until all the water from the onions and tomatoes evaporates, mix occasionally.
  7. When the mixture is all dry, add small pieces of skinned chicken (no bones).
  8. Add 1 spoon red chili pepper, 1 spoon salt, and ½ spoon turmeric.
  9. Cook and stir for 10 minutes.
  10. Add 2 cups heavy whipping cream.
  11. When it starts boiling, it’s ready.
  12. Taste to see if salt and pepper is fine.
  13. Serve with rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 onions chopped
  • 4 tomatoes chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp red chili pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 chicken breasts cubed
  • ½ cup oil
